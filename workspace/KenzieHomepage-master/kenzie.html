    Meet Kenzie

    We founded Kenxie Academy to tackle a major opportunities gap in Amaerica. We discovered that fast-growing technology companies concentrated in exspensive coastal cities were struggling to hire tech talent. Meanwhile, a large population of aspiring individuals across the country could help fuel these talent needs. Our mission is to equip this population with th necessary skills, experience, and access in order to build successful careers in tech.
